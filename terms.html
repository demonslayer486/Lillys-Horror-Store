Terms of Service Agreement for Art Commission

1. Turnaround Time

- The standard turnaround time is 1 to 2 months from the payment date for general commissions.

- Delays in payment or feedback will result in a corresponding delay in turnaround time.

- Period subject to change based on commission type and queue status.

2. Revisions

- Adjustments are available during the commission progress.

- Please communicate all changes at once to minimize revisions.

3. Payment

- All prices are in USD, payment via PayPal invoice.

- Upfront payment is required to initiate the commission.

4. Refunds

- Refunds are not applicable once the invoice is paid.

- Refunds may be offered if:

  • The commission is in progress and around the sketch phase.
  • The artist feels unable to meet the commissioner's standards.

- Please seek approval before reselling commissioned work.

5. Can/ Cannot Draw

- Can draw: OCs, fanart, in-game characters, ecchi, kemonomimi, humanoid characters.

- Cannot draw: Heavy mecha, gore, full-out NSFW, certain themes/genres.

- Gore or NSFW requests may be considered with caution, and the artist reserves the right to refuse requests deemed excessively grotesque.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Lilly's Horror Shop ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website to commission an artist.

Information We Collect

- Name: We may collect your name if you choose to provide it.

- Email Address: We may collect your email address if you choose to provide it.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate the commissioning process.
  • Communicate with you about your commission.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve our website and services.

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With artists you choose to commission, to facilitate communication and completion of your project.

Data Retention

We will retain your name and email address only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. If you request that your information be deleted, we will do so within 30 days of receiving your request.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Request that we delete your personal information.
